Welcome to A Bit of Love Foster Care

At A Bit of Love, we provide round-the-clock care for Idaho's children in need. Our nurturing environment, guided by state standards, ensures stability, nutrition, and essential support. With trauma-informed care and integrated mental health services, we empower children to heal and thrive. Our goal is to make a lasting impact and facilitate successful transitions to loving homes.
